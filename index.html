Pizza Sales
Analysis

In this Power BI dashboard, I've conducted a thorough analysis of pizza sales for a pizza store. The dashboard features key insights such as the top and bottom 5 pizzas based on revenue and quantity, total orders, and KPIs related to pizza categories. Users can explore sales trends on a daily and monthly basis, analyze the percentage of sales by pizza category and size, and view the total number of pizzas sold in each category.
The dashboard also highlights the busiest days and times, providing valuable information for optimizing operations and enhancing decision-making. With interactive features allowing for time selection, users can customize their exploration of the data for a more tailored and insightful experience.

Call Center
Analysis

In this Power BI dashboard, I've performed an in-depth analysis of a call center's operations. The dashboard includes key metrics such as total calls on a daily basis, the distribution of calls across different communication channels, the geographical breakdown of calls by state, and the analysis of sentiments expressed during these interactions. Furthermore, the dashboard delves into specific details such as calls segmented by different call centers, reasons for the calls, and the associated dates.

To enhance user interactivity and customization, I've incorporated filters for date, channel, and city, allowing users to dynamically explore and analyze the call center data based on their specific criteria and areas of interest.
